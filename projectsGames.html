    Office Dash
    Office Dash is an infinite runner built using UE4. In this game you play as a disgruntled office worker who is tired of his 9-5 job. You decide to quit your job dramatically by leaping out of the closest window to run across the rooftops. Along the way you collect employee of the month trophies to increase your high score during your run.
